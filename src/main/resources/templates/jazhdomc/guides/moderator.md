## Role
- Enforce the rules.
- Do not abuse your role or abilities.
- Make sure to **UNBAN** players after their punishment time is over.
- Log any and all of your moderation in the section of the dashboard titled "Mod Log".
- Be a role model for all other players.
- Review the rules and coresponding punishments before moderating anyone for anything.
